Trevalgan Touring Park

11 August 2021 Journal
Back to Journal

Stone Sign Company were recently approached by Trevalgan Touring Park regarding some stone business signage that would complement the natural surroundings of the park.

Trevalgan is a luxury family camping and touring park located near St Ives, offering stunning coastal and countryside views. The Park also presents five-star on-site facilities and were seeking some wayfinding signage that would reflect these elements whilst also catching the eye.

They reached out to us with some initial ideas which our specialist team were swiftly able to bring to life. The specifications were to include a larger sign coming in at just under two metres tall, featuring as the entrance sign to the park, as well as multiple other wayfinding plaques to locate all other facilities on site.

A selection of locally sourced Welsh slate was then chosen, perfect for this project as it can retain its natural beauty for years to come, whilst being able to withstand outdoor weathering. This is ideal for the Trevalgan Touring Park as the business is located close to the sea.

Each plaque was deeply engraved, and carefully hand painted using a white enamel infill, contrasting beautifully with the dark blue-black tones of natural Welsh slate. The entrance sign featured Trevalgan's intricate logo, which was expertly hand painted to ensure that all the finer details were captured. All plaques were then fitted with faceless fixings, ensuring that no drill holes would interrupt the design.
