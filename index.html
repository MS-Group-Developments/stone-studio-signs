What we do

Stone signage
for the buildings
that mean something.

The Stone Studio │ Signs division supplies business signage where the material — and the quality of the making — is the point. Not a shortcut to permanence: the actual thing.

We work with hospitality groups, corporate facilities teams, property developers and design studios. We pick up at whatever stage suits you: brief only, or a locked drawing ready to cut.

Every piece is produced in our Cardiff workshop, by the same hands that have been cutting Welsh stone since 1821. Specification, fabrication and installation under one roof.

Services

What we make.

01

Business Name Plaques

Hand-cut or photo-blasted name plaques in Welsh slate, Portland limestone, granite and sandstone. Gilded, painted or natural finish. Wall-mounted or freestanding.

02

Façade Signage

Corporate façade lettering and branded signage suites. Designed to work with your building's material palette — stone-to-stone or stone-to-metal combinations.

03

Wayfinding Suites

Consistent directional and identification signage across a development or campus. One material specification, one supplier, one point of contact throughout.

04

Reception & Interior

Entrance wall panels, reception desk lettering, directional plaques and floor-inlaid identification features. Interior-grade finishes available throughout.
