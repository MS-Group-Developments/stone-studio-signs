Stone signage for residential, commercial and civic projects.

Signs

Made to mark.
Built to last.

From a single house name to a hotel entrance, we design and produce stone signage that earns its place on a building for generations. Carved, sandblasted, gilded or cast — every technique, one workshop.

We work directly with architects, developers and private clients. Supply-only or fully installed. Across the UK and internationally.

Services

What we make

Architectural name plates

Building name panels, entrance stones and facade-mounted signage for commercial and residential developments.

House names & numbers

Carved slate, limestone and granite for private residences. Bespoke lettering, standard or gilded finish.

Sandblasted & gilded lettering

Deep-cut sandblasted text with paint or gold-leaf infill. The most durable letter technique available in stone.

Wayfinding & directional

Multi-panel wayfinding systems for campuses, estates, hotels and public realm. Stone with longevity beyond any other material.

Opening plaques

Foundation stones, opening plaques and building record panels. Permanent, dignified and made to outlast the building.

Commemorative plaques

Wall-mounted and freestanding plaques for commemorations, royal visits and civic ceremonies.

Sustainably Sourced

British-quarried natural stone contributes up to 304% less carbon than imported alternatives — we work exclusively with UK quarries.
